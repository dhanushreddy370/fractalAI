**1. Strategy Name & Core Concept**

**Momentum Sweep Scalper**
A high-frequency scalping strategy that exploits EUR/USD's strong tendency for efficient directional movement by entering on confirmed momentum breaks following liquidity sweeps at key historical support/resistance levels. The strategy capitalizes on the high probability of continuation after price decisively interacts with previous session highs/lows and daily/weekly extremes.

**2. Market & Timeframe**

- **Market:** EUR/USD
- **Primary Timeframe:** M15 (for entry/exit execution)
- **Analysis Timeframes:** H1, H4, Daily (for level identification)

**3. Statistical Foundation**

The strategy is built exclusively on the following OHLC-derived statistical evidence from the Market Profile:

- **87.51% efficient trend probability on M15** provides the core premise: momentum continuation is highly reliable intra-candle.
- **Low reversal (11.10%) and indecision (1.39%) rates on M15** justify trading with the prevailing momentum rather than anticipating reversals.
- **Prev_D_Low shows 47.72% liquidity sweep probability** indicating this level frequently attracts short-term stop hunts before continuation.
- **Prev_W_High shows 48.68% liquidity sweep probability** making it a prime level for momentum initiation.
- **Prev_D_High shows 43.29% breach-and-continue probability** suggesting strong follow-through after breakouts.
- **Prev_D_Low shows only 11.53% rejection probability** confirming daily lows are weak reversal points and strong continuation triggers.

**4. Entry Rules**

**Long Entry Conditions (ALL must be met):**
1. Price must approach within 0.0005 of a predefined key level from below: Prev_D_Low, Prev_W_High, or Prev_D_High
2. Price must sweep the level (trade through it by minimum 0.0002) then show rejection candle (close back above level)
3. Next candle must open and trade above the rejection candle's high
4. Entry triggered at break of rejection candle high + 0.0001 buffer

**Short Entry Conditions (ALL must be met):**
1. Price must approach within 0.0005 of a predefined key level from above: Prev_D_High, Prev_W_High, or Prev_Session_High
2. Price must sweep the level (trade through it by minimum 0.0002) then show rejection candle (close back below level)
3. Next candle must open and trade below the rejection candle's low
4. Entry triggered at break of rejection candle low - 0.0001 buffer

**Prohibited Conditions:**
- No entries during first/last 30 minutes of trading session
- No entries if previous M15 candle showed >50% range retracement (indecision)
- No entries if price is within 0.0010 of daily open (neutral zone)

**5. Exit Rules**

**Profit Target:**
- Fixed 0.0015 from entry for all trades
- Based on M15 average efficient move length derived from profile data

**Stop Loss:**
- Initial stop: 0.0008 from entry
- Stop moved to breakeven + 0.0001 after price moves 0.0005 in favor
- Trail stop using 0.0004 buffer below/above most recent M15 swing low/high

**Time-based Exit:**
- Close all positions after 4 M15 candles (1 hour) regardless of outcome

**6. Risk Management**

**Position Sizing:**
- Maximum risk per trade: 0.25% of account equity
- Position size = (0.0025 * Account Equity) / (Entry - Stop Distance)

**Daily Risk Caps:**
- Maximum 3 trades per day
- Daily maximum loss: 1.0% of account equity
- Trading suspended after 2 consecutive losing trades

**Session Constraints:**
- No trading during major news events (30 minutes before/after)
- No trading during low liquidity periods (22:00-02:00 GMT)

**7. Key Performance Metrics**

**Backtest Calculation Methodology:**

**Profit Factor:** Total Gross Profit / Total Gross Loss
- Gross Profit = Sum of all winning trade P&L
- Gross Loss = Sum of all losing trade P&L (absolute values)

**Win Rate:** (Number of Winning Trades / Total Trades) * 100

**Max Drawdown:** 
- Calculate running equity curve peak-to-trough decline
- Maximum peak-to-trough percentage decline during backtest period

**Average Win/Loss Ratio:** 
- Average winning trade profit / Average losing trade loss

**Expectancy:** (Win Rate * Average Win) - (Loss Rate * Average Loss)
