### Market Profile: EUR/USD

**Section 1: Market Character Summary**

The EUR/USD pair exhibits a strong tendency toward efficient directional movement across multiple timeframes. Intra-candle momentum is predominantly fast and decisive, with a high probability of price continuing in the current direction without significant reversals. This behavior is most pronounced at shorter timeframes (M15, H1) but remains consistent even at higher intervals (H4). The market displays low indecision, indicating that once momentum is established, it is likely to persist with minimal hesitation.
